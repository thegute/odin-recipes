Parmesan Baked Salmon

This is a easy baked salmon recipe that I like to make when salmon is on sale.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mayonaise
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Rinse salmon and pat dry
  2. salt and pepper your salmon
  3. spread a light coat of mayonaise on the fish
  4. sprinkle Parmesan cheese on the mayonaise
  5. bake at 400 degrees F or until cheese is golden brown
