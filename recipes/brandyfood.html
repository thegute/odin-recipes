Brandy's Food

This is the recipe to make Brandy's Food. She eats it every day for breakfast and dinner. She really loves that we mix both the dry and the wet food together into a special dish. For as long as we have had her she has not once complained about this dish. I hope you can ejoy it as much as she does.

Ingredients

  • Dry Dog Food
  • Wet Dog Food

Steps

  1. Using a measuring cup scoop 2 Cups of dry dogfood into the bowl.
  2. Grab your can of wet dog food and scoop 2 Tablespoons into the bowl.
  3. Thoroughly mix and combine both ingredients in dog bowl.
  4. Enjoy!
