Meat Jun

Meat Jun is a delicious combonation of teriyaki beef dipped and fried in an egg.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound lean beef, thinly sliced
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt and pepper
  • Flour for dredging
  • Vegetable oil to pan fry
  • soy sauce

Directions:

  1. Cut beef into 2 inch by 2 inch pieces. if beef is thick, pound meat with the back ofa knife ore meat tenderizer to make it paper thin.
  2. Lightly season meat with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat non-stick skillet with 2 to 3 tablespoons of oil
  4. Dredge the meat in the flour, shaking off any excess flour, then dip into egg
  5. Pan fry the meat for aobut a minute on each side or until lightly browned.
  6. Serve with soy sauce. Makes 2 servings
